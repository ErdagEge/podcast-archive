About Açık Büfe Diyalog

Welcome to the Açık Büfe Diyalog Podcast Archive! Here you can learn more about our story, our mission, and the people behind the mic.

Our Mission

Açık Büfe Diyalog is a podcast dedicated to deep, honest, and sometimes humorous conversations about culture, society, art, and the world around us. We believe in the power of open dialogue and critical thinking, and we aim to create a space where ideas can be explored freely and thoughtfully.

The Hosts

Ege Erdağ and friends bring you each episode, sharing their perspectives, stories, and curiosity. With backgrounds in culture, media, and storytelling, the team is passionate about making complex topics accessible and engaging for everyone.

Why This Archive?

This website is a home for all our episodes, show notes, and extra content. Whether you're a new listener or a long-time fan, you can browse, search, and enjoy every conversation we've had so far.

Contact

Want to get in touch, suggest a topic, or just say hi? Reach out via egeerdag@gmail.com or follow us on Instagram.
